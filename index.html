Specialized Search

Let Dandelion do the legwork and find the best qualified match for you. With your specific surrogate or egg/sperm donor requirements in mind, and through our network of 50+ trusted agencies, you will have access to a broad pool of high quality candidates, making you feel confident that you are making the right choice. Our Specialized Search process is fast and secure.

This package includes fast matching times, a dedicated matching manager, creation of your intended parent profile, fully screened candidate profiles, expert advice and coaching during the selection process, a team advocating for you in every match meeting, communications with the agencies for follow-up questions and facilitation of a thorough medical record review with your fertility doctor.

Full-Service Advocacy Program

While navigating through the often cumbersome and complex surrogate or egg donor process, with a lot of moving elements and parties involved, having a dedicated team in your corner to advocate for you ensures total piece of mind. Our Full-Service Advocacy Program is journey based and can be tailored to your personal needs. Through the Dandelion Advocacy Framework™, we provide ongoing expertise, guidance and support during the full lifecycle of the journey, giving you more control. 

This all-inclusive package includes the Specialized Search package, complete versight and management of the entire journey from a birth’s eye view, a edicated program manager, continuous advocating services every step of the journey, access to our network of top specialists in the field such as medical, legal, insurance and mental health experts, all communications with parties  involved including your surrogacy agency to make sure everything is taken care of, acting as a mediator if issues arise, guidance and best practices on how to build a relationship with your surrogate, ensuring all appropriate insurances are in place or your surrogate such as medical, accidental death, lost wages and disability  insurances, all medical elements are arranged for like the delivery hospital,  birthing plan and any medical bill negotiation, and our full support all the way from matching to your baby’s birth and beyond.
